All About Me

I am Josh Carroccia and I am from a small town in South Central Montana called Melville. I grew up on a ranch nestled deep into the Crazy Mountains where I enjoyed camping, hiking, fishing and riding horses.

I have been working in the tech industry for 3 years in various roles. I was a Team Lead for a software implentation team at a company called PFL. In that role I learned how to lead a technical team and work with teams across the company to create and develop great solutions for our customers. At that role I also found my appreciation and passion toward coding. There we used javascript to create dynamic marketing materials for our clients.

Most recently I worked for Oracle as a Marketing Analyst, where I built emails and landing pages for the marketing teams across North America. Between PFL and Oracle, I realized that I wanted to build and work with more complex projects.

I moved out to Durham because of the strong tech community that is continuing to grow and figured this would be a great place to pursue a future in development. Therefore, I took the leap and joined Momentum Learning's Web Development Bootcamp. Since I had some front-end experience, I decided to choose the backend development route in order to gain as much knowledge as possible.

Outside of coding I enjoy binge watching tv shows, playing first person shooters and RPGs and playing with my dog, Zeke.
